Working framework

Clear terms, from the start

  • No-pressure requests — requests can come at any time, with no urgency imposed on either side.
  • Limited capacity — we work on a small number of projects to ensure our availability.
  • Per-project billing — billing terms are agreed together at the start of each engagement.
  • No emergency support — we do not handle last-minute requests.
  • Transparent terms — everything is clearly defined before we start, with no surprises.
